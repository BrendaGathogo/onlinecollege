Our Blog

AcadeWe offers a 6-month full-time program to train backend engineers. Graduates will be prepared with the skills necessary to land entry-level software developer roles at companies of all sizes. Our curriculum is jointly developed by BloomTech and Amazon, and is based on critical knowledge, skills, and attributes required to succeed as a backend engineer. Each graduate of the new Enterprise Backend Development program will leave AcadeWe with over 1,300 hours of hands-on coding experience and will have the practical skills required to succeed at companies like Microsoft, without needing a 4-year Bachelors degree. Our curriculum covers the following topics and more: - Java Programming - Data Structures and Algorithms - Software Engineering - Testing - DynamoDB - Concurrency - Interview Preparation
